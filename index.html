National Museum Delhi

The National Museum has an extensive series of anthropological artifacts that include items such as cloth and other materials, pottery, basketry, wood, metal, and paper. The museum has in its possession over 200,000 works of art, of both Indian and foreign origin. While the National Museum has over 10,000 exhibits, it currently displays six to seven percent of its collection
